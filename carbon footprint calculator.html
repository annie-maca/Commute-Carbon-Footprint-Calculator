    Emissions data

    Air quality & health

    • NOₓ and ozone — EPA Integrated Science Assessment for Oxides of Nitrogen; nitrogen oxides contribute to ground-level ozone (smog) formation. epa.gov/air-research
    • PM2.5 and health — EPA health and environmental effects of particulate matter; fine particles from tailpipes can penetrate lungs and affect respiratory and cardiovascular health. epa.gov/pm-pollution
